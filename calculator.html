Questions

Frequently asked

What is a U-100 syringe?
A U-100 insulin syringe measures 100 units per millilitre. Our calculator converts your dose into these units so you can draw precisely.
What is reconstitution volume?
It is the amount of bacteriostatic water you add to the lyophilised peptide powder. More water means a lower concentration and more units per dose.
Does the calculator store my data?
No. Everything is calculated locally in your browser — nothing is saved or sent anywhere.
Can I use it for any peptide?
Yes. Enter any custom strength, dose and diluent volume, and the calculator returns the exact units to draw.
