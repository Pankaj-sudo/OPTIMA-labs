# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Women (roughly 28–55) investing in their own wellness — the medspa / boutique-skincare / hormone-health clientele. They are discerning, research their purchases, and expect premium presentation backed by real credibility (purity, testing, provenance). They arrive from Instagram, referrals, or clinic recommendations, usually on mobile.

## Product Purpose

OPTIMA Labs sells pharmaceutical-grade peptides delivered as a complete system: a >99% pure vial, a pre-filled cartridge, and a reusable auto-injector pen ("OptiPen"). The landing page must make the product feel like a considered self-care ritual — not a lab supply purchase — while keeping the science credentials (purity, third-party COAs, cold-chain) visible and verifiable. Success = visitor trusts the brand enough to buy or book.

## Brand Personality

Calm, elegant, confidence-inspiring. The visual world of a high-end medspa, boutique skincare line, or luxury wellness retreat. Voice: warm, assured, unhurried — speaks to self-investment, never fear or hype. Three words: **graceful, credible, warm**.

## Anti-references

- Dark biotech / cyberpunk (the previous identity: slate #0F172A + neon cyan). Explicitly retired.
- "Tech startup" aesthetics: geometric sans everywhere, gradient glows, terminal-mono labels.
- Clinical-cold hospital white; anything that feels like a pharmacy shelf.
- Bro-supplement / gym-stack branding.

## Design Principles

1. **Ritual over mechanism.** Motion and copy read like a skincare ritual — graceful, unhurried — never a mechanical assembly line.
2. **Credibility whispers.** Purity, testing, and COAs are always present but rendered as refined details (fine gold rules, quiet type), never shouted badges.
3. **Softness with spine.** Rounded/organic shapes, soft shadows, warm gradients — grounded by espresso ink and confident serif headlines so it never turns saccharine.
4. **No unverified health claims.** Copy speaks to quality, precision, and ritual; never to medical outcomes.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Body text ≥4.5:1 contrast on tinted backgrounds (espresso ink on porcelain).
- Full `prefers-reduced-motion` alternative for the hero sequence (crossfade, complete pen shown).
- Mobile-first responsiveness; hero animation simplified on small screens.
